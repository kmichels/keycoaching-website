About Key Coaching

Empowering Tech Careers in Changing Times

Welcome to Key Coaching. I'm Konrad Michels, the founder and principal coach behind this practice dedicated to empowering tech professionals to navigate and thrive in their careers.

My Journey

With 30 years in Tech, and over 12 years of experience at Meta (Facebook), I've witnessed firsthand the dynamic and often unpredictable nature of the tech industry. I was the first technical hire outside the US at Facebook, starting in London in 2008 and building out the European SRE presence from scratch.

I've held multiple roles - Lead SRE, Manager, Production Engineering, TPM, and Product Manager. I've led major initiatives like Project Sandstorm (the first-ever planned datacenter shutdown without user impact), managed cache infrastructure serving tens of billions of requests per second, and built enterprise video conferencing systems.

This journey has given me a unique perspective on what it takes to build a resilient and fulfilling tech career. It's this experience that fuels my passion for coaching and drives me to help others unlock their full potential.

Why I Started Key Coaching

In my years in the tech industry, I saw too many talented professionals struggle with:

  • Unexpected career transitions and layoffs
  • Feeling stuck or undervalued in their current roles
  • Navigating toxic work environments
  • Uncertainty about how to advance their careers

I realized that my experience and insights could help bridge the gap between where tech professionals are and where they want to be. That's why I founded Key Coaching - to provide the guidance, strategies, and support that I wish I had earlier in my career.
