About Key Coaching

Empowering Tech Careers in Changing Times

Welcome to Key Coaching. I'm Konrad Michels, the founder and principal coach behind this practice dedicated to empowering tech professionals to navigate and thrive in their careers.

My Journey

With over 25 years in Tech, and over 12 years of experience as a tech professional at a FAANG company, I've witnessed firsthand the dynamic and often unpredictable nature of the tech industry. I've been through the highs of rapid growth and innovation, and I've navigated the challenges of restructuring and market shifts.

This journey has given me a unique perspective on what it takes to build a resilient and fulfilling tech career. It's this experience that fuels my passion for coaching and drives me to help others unlock their full potential in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

Why I Started Key Coaching

In my years in the tech industry, I saw too many talented professionals struggle with:

  • Unexpected career transitions and layoffs
  • Feeling stuck or undervalued in their current roles
  • Navigating toxic work environments
  • Uncertainty about how to advance their careers

I realized that my experience and insights could help bridge the gap between where tech professionals are and where they want to be. That's why I founded Key Coaching - to provide the guidance, strategies, and support that I wish I had earlier in my career.

My Coaching Philosophy

At Key Coaching, I believe that:

Unique Talents

Every tech professional has unique talents waiting to be unlocked.

Growth Opportunities

Career setbacks can be transformed into opportunities for growth.

Continuous Learning

Continuous learning and adaptation are key to long-term success in tech.

Right Guidance

With the right guidance, anyone can navigate the complexities of the tech job market.

My approach combines practical industry insights, proven career advancement strategies, and personalized coaching to help you achieve your professional goals.

What Sets Key Coaching Apart

  • Deep Industry Knowledge: My FAANG background gives me insider insights into what top tech companies look for.
  • Holistic Approach: I focus not just on job placement, but on overall career satisfaction and growth.
  • Adaptability: My strategies evolve with the job market, ensuring you're always prepared for what's next.
  • Empathy and Support: Having navigated career challenges myself, I provide not just strategic advice, but also the emotional support needed during transitions.

Who I Work With

I partner with a diverse range of tech professionals, including:

  • Engineers, Program Managers, and others looking to level up their careers
  • Tech leaders aiming to enhance their management skills
  • Professionals facing unexpected career transitions or layoffs
  • Those seeking to navigate away from toxic work environments

No matter where you are in your tech career journey, I'm here to help you clarify your path, overcome obstacles, and achieve your professional aspirations.
