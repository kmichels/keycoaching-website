Services

At Key Coaching, I offer a range of services designed to meet you wherever you are in your tech career journey. Whether you're navigating a job transition, seeking advancement, or looking to make a strategic career move, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

My Coaching Process

  1. Initial Consultation - 30 minutes, $25
    • Meet me and discuss your current situation and goals
    • Get a taste of my coaching approach and how I can help
    • $25.00 fee applied as credit toward Discovery Session or other package if you chose to continue.
  2. In-depth Discovery Session - 1 hour, $125.00
    • Dive deep into your skills, experiences, and career aspirations
    • Receive a high-level proposed plan tailored to your needs
    • $25 of fee refunded toward coaching package should you chose to continue
  3. Personalized Coaching Sessions
    • Choose from a range of flexible packages to suit your needs and budget
    • Schedule your first session and start your journey
  4. Your Personal Coaching Portal
    • Access worksheets, courses, and progress-tracking tools
    • Communicate directly with me between sessions
  5. On-going Support and Accountability
    • Regular check-ins to ensure you're on track with your goals
    • Adjust your plan as needed based on your progress and changing circumstances

My Core Services

  1. Career Transition Coaching: perfect for those dealing with layoffs, seeking to exit toxic work environments, or looking to make a strategic career change.
  2. Job Search Acceleration: designed to help you land your next role as quickly and efficiently as possible.
  3. Career Advancement Coaching: for professionals looking to grow within their current role or company
  4. Tech Skill Development Planning: stay ahead in the fast-paced tech industry.
  5. Long-Term Career Strategy: develop a comprehensive plan for long-term success.
