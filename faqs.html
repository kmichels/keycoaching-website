Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tech career coaching

What does a tech career coach do?

A tech career coach provides specialized guidance to help you navigate and advance your career in the technology industry. I work with you to develop career strategies specific to tech, optimize your job search process including resume crafting and interview preparation, and guide you in developing the skills necessary to stay competitive. I also provide support for career transitions, including dealing with layoffs or leaving toxic work environments, and help develop leadership and management skills for tech professionals.

How can tech career coaching help me in today's job market?

In today's rapidly changing tech landscape, career coaching can be an invaluable resource. I help you stay ahead of industry trends and identify in-demand skills, ensuring you remain competitive. My coaching assists you in developing strategies to stand out in a crowded job market and navigate unexpected career transitions or layoffs with confidence. I work with you to optimize your job search process, potentially reducing the time it takes to secure a new role. Moreover, I focus on building your resilience and confidence, essential qualities for long-term success in the dynamic tech industry.

Do you guarantee job placement or salary increases?

While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, as success ultimately depends on various factors including individual effort and market conditions, my clients consistently report significant improvements in their job search success and overall career satisfaction. My coaching is designed to substantially increase your chances of achieving your career goals. I provide you with the tools, strategies, and insights needed to excel in your tech career journey.

What makes Key Coaching different from other career coaching services?

Key Coaching stands out due to several unique factors. I bring deep industry expertise gained from over 30 years in the industry, including 12+ years of experience at Meta, providing insider insights that are invaluable in today's competitive tech landscape. My approach is holistic, considering not just your career aspirations but your overall life goals. I offer customized strategies tailored to the unique challenges of the tech industry, going beyond generic career advice. My services are flexible, addressing both immediate job search needs and long-term career development.

How long does the coaching process typically last?

My coaching engagements are flexible and tailored to your specific needs and goals. I offer a range of options, from single-session consultations for specific issues to longer-term engagements for comprehensive career development. My short-term packages, like the 4-session Career Transition Jumpstart, are ideal for focused, immediate needs. For those looking for more extensive support, I offer longer-term engagements such as our 4.5 hours/month Comprehensive Career Makeover package. I also provide ongoing monthly support for continuous career guidance. Typically, I recommend starting with a 3-month engagement to establish momentum and see significant progress.

What is an Income Share Agreement (ISA), and how does it work?

An Income Share Agreement is a flexible payment option I offer, designed to align my success with yours. Under this agreement, you pay for coaching services as a percentage of your income once you secure a job, rather than paying upfront fees. My Career Accelerator ISA package includes up to 4.5 hours of coaching per month for 6 months, with no upfront cost. You only begin payments as a percentage of your new salary for a fixed period once you're employed, and I cap the total amount paid to ensure fairness. Full terms and conditions, including eligibility criteria, are available upon request.

How do I know if I'm ready for career coaching?

Tech career coaching can be beneficial at various stages of your career. You might find it particularly valuable if you're feeling stuck or unsatisfied in your current role, facing a career transition or unexpected layoff, or aspiring to advance to a leadership position in tech. It's also highly beneficial if you're looking to pivot to a different area within the tech industry, need help optimizing your job search in a competitive market, or are early in your tech career and want to establish a strong foundation. If you're unsure, I offer an initial consultation to discuss your situation and determine if coaching is the right step for you.
